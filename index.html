Haiku-Fi

Haiku-Fi is a simple Haiku (Haiku is a special kind of poem) detector and it saves valid "haiku". Here, we don't follow the exact rules of the Haiku,
But rather, just the essentials , i.e. any sentence made of three lines, first line with 5 words, second line with 7 words third line with 5 words,
is considered a Haiku. The user may also view the Haikus along with the authors' names. Also, One doesn't need to register to get their haiku saved, just provide your username.
